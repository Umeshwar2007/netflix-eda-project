# Netflix EDA Project

## Overview
Exploratory Data Analysis on Netflix Movies & TV Shows dataset

## Dataset Source
[Netflix Dataset - Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shivamb/netflix-shows)

## Project Includes
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- 5 EDA questions answered with Pandas
- 6+ visualizations (pie, bar, line, histogram, heatmap, scatter)
- 5 business insights based on analysis

## Key Statistics
- Total titles analyzed: 8,794
- Movies: 69.7% | TV Shows: 30.3%
- Top country: USA with 2,555 titles
- Most common rating: TV-MA

## Most Surprising Finding
### Findings
From the exploratory data analysis, I found that Netflix's content library is mainly made up of movies, with movies accounting for nearly 70% of the total titles. The USA has the highest number of titles on the platform, while India is the second-largest contributor, showing Netflix's strong focus on regional content. I also observed a significant increase in content added during 2021, which may be linked to the increased demand for streaming services during the COVID-19 pandemic. Another interesting finding is that international movies and TV shows form a large part of Netflix's catalog, highlighting the platform's strategy of reaching audiences across different countries and languages.
